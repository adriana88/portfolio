Adriana Wojtanowska

I am a BI, Marketing & Data Specialist with 5+ years of professional experience.

At Wolf Theiss, a leading Austrian law firm, I built stable data models and developed interactive Power BI dashboards focused on strategic marketing KPIs. Additionally, I executed complex SQL queries , provided senior lawyers with ad-hoc BI reports , and was one of the primary CRM administrators overseeing the entire contact database. Furthermore, I led the implementation of new digital marketing software and managed campaigns and online content.

Since April 2024, I have been serving as a Data Analyst for a major Austrian company in the building & construction industry where (so far) I have created a complex data model and a comprehensive, fully interactive Power BI dashboard integrating complex SQL queries.
